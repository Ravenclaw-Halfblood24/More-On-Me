Hobbies/Passions/Interests

What occupies the time and mind of this young teenager? Let's find out!

My ideal day would include a day out with Mother Nature, in the dense forest, mystery/adventure book in hand, pop songs playing in the background. I would be up in the trees in a treehouse, sitting and reading the book, writing a story or poem, or working on song lyrics. I would dance to the song of the wild along with the trees, and animals in the woods. Nature is my happy place, a place where you can be free and not feel afraid of being judged for being who you are. Mother Nature doesn't judge, she accepts like a loving mother always does. Being out with nature, and hearing the calming sounds of the birds chirping, and the feel of the wind against face all brings a sense of calm and freedom. It's a great place to sit down and wrestle with your thoughts, or do just the opposite, cast away your thoughts and have a moment of peace and quiet.

Many kids have their lives all figured out, and know their goals and what they want to do in life, but that's not me. I'm generally an indecisive person. Yay! Lucky me! (<--NOTE THE SARCASM HERE) I have hobbies and passions, but I don;t have just one set job that stands out to me where I can say, "That's it. That's what I want to do in life. It's what I'm meant to do." However, I do have a couple jobs in mind. The first being a dectective or a federal agent. (NCIS, CIA, FBI that kind of road). I love solving mysteries, and helping bring justice. I may not know what I want to do for sure in life yet, but I want to be able to help people. Being a detective or agent, will help me bring justice to innocent people, and give the guilty the punishment they deserve. Another job in consideration is a lawayer. The way this job came to mind is actually a funny story. I love arguing, (he he... oops ;) and so my mom suggested I put my arguing skills to good use. In this way, I would also be able to help protect the innocent and fight for things I believe in using the law to my advantage. Straying from the path of law, I would also maybe like to be a chemist or scientist when I grow up. I love science, and throughout school it has always my favorite subject. This job would allow me to maybe use my knowledge and passion for science to help find cures for incurable diseases to date or make scientific advancements that could revolutionize the world.
